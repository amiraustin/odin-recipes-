Shrimp Pasta

garlic shrimp pasta is an elegant pasta dish with big flavors! It's so quick and easy, and perfect for a weeknight dinner.

ingredients list

  1. Shrimp – Use good quality raw frozen shrimp that is peeled, or peel and devein it yourself. Any size of shrimp works here as long as you don’t overcook it.
  2. Olive oil, butter, and garlic – this is where most of the flavor comes from
  3. Pasta – any long strand pasta of choice, I went for linguine.
  4. Cream – heavy cream (double cream in the UK) works best here, the fat content of heavy cream is emulsified with the reserved pasta cooking water, and creates a beautiful silky cream sauce that clings into the past
  5. armesan – get the best parmesan that you can get your hands on! It gives you that umami/savory flavor that you want in the sauce, and it also helps thicken the cream sauce
